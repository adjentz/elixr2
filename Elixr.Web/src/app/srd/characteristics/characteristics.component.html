Characteristics

Characteristics are traits that make your character unique or interesting, and often grant a creature special abilities. They may be supernatural in nature, in which case, you should describe how your character acquired these traits.

They fall in one of three categories: Features, Flaws, and Oaths, each detailed on their own page.

Every Characteristic has an associated Power indicated by a number next to the icon. Beginning at first level, a character has 8 Feature Points with which to take on Features. In order to gain a Feature, you spend points equal to the power of the Feature.

Some Flaws and Oaths actually have a negative Power. Negative Power values increase a character's number of Feature Points available to compensate. So a character can take these characteristics to increase their Featue Points.

For example, Rorric at level 1 has 8 Feature Points. He spends 1 of them to get Speak Language, leaving him with 7 Feature Points. He then takes on the Flaw Deaf, which has a Power equal to -5. His Feature points is now 12. (8 to start minus 1 for a Feature with a Power of 1 plus 5 for a Flaw with a Power of -5).

Each level, a character gains 8 Feature Points. A character's Feature Points should never go below 0.
