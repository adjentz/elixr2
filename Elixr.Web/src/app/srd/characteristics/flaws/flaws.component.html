Flaws

Flaws are traits that detract from a character's effectiveness or personality.

Some Flaws have positive trade offs. For example, a blind character has Advantage on Perception checks that relate to sound, at the cost of being unable to make Perception checks based off of sight at all.

Other Flaws serve simply to make a character more interesting. Characters that are perfect are often seen as unrealistic, or worse, boring.
