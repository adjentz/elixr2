World Building

Where does your story take place? Likely, it's a completely different world, or realm, than our own. While not necessary, you may want to flesh it out. Here are some ideas to help you do so.

A Map

A drawing of where events occur can help players visualize your world.

Planes of Existence

Elixr is built with the idea that creatures live on one of 8 "planes" and that these planes overlap and can be traversed through magic. The planes are:

  • Fire — Where creatures such Fire Elementals exist.
  • Air — Where creatures such as Air Elementals exist.
  • Earth — Where creatures such as Earth Elementals exist.
  • Water — Where creatures such as Water Elementals exist.
  • Infernal — Where demons exist.
  • Celestial — Where angels exist.
  • Astral — Where spectral creatures such as ghosts exist. This plane overlaps with the Material Plane.
  • Material — where most creatures such humans, elves, dwarves, etc... exist.

On the Astral Plane, creatures have no physical form. The Material and Astral planes overlap the most, almost exactly, in fact.

Of course, you're free to make your own multiverse topography, what's included in Elixr is just an example.

Deities

What Gods exist in this world? Do you have one for each Race, perhaps? Or maybe there's a God for every natural phenomenon. It's up to you.

History and Culture

What wars have been fought? What bloodlines claim power? Who are the movers and shakers of the world?
