Exploration

As you explore the world, it's likely that you'll run into some environmental dangers. Some rules for dealing with these are detailed below.

Falling

When a creature falls more than 10ft, they will take Damage once they hit the ground. This table indicates how much damage is given for differnt heights:

Fall Height Damage
10ft 0
20ft 1d4
30ft 1d4 + 1d6
40ft 1d4 + 1d6 + 1d8
50ft 1d4 + 1d6 + 1d8 + 1d10
60ft 1d4 + 1d6 + 1d8 + 1d10 + 1d12
70ft 1d4 + 1d6 + 1d8 + 1d10 + 1d12 + 1d20
80ft+ Fatal

If a creature intentionally jumps, the fall height is considered to be 10ft less than it actually was for Damage purposes.

If a creature didn't intentionally jump, but makes an Acrobatics check that exceeds 10 + 1 for every 10ft fallen, the fall height is considered to be 10ft less than it actually was for Damage purposes.

If a creature lands in water, the fall height is considered to be 10ft less than it actually was for Damage purposes. This stacks on top of the reduction offered by a successful acrobatics check or intentional jump, if any.

If a creature lands on another creature, the fall damage is split between the two creatures evenly.

Drowning & Suffocating

Creatures can stay without access to air for 1 round per their Strength Score. After that, for every round that they cannot breathe, they lose 1 Energy at the start of their turn.

Thirst & Starvation

A character can go without water for a number of days equal to their level.

A character can go without eating for a number of days equal to their level * 2.

Once a character has gone longer than mentioned without food or water, their Max Energy is reduced by the amount of days they have gone without sustenance.

For example, a level 3 character with Energy of 16 goes without water for 7 days. At the start of the 4th day, their Max Energy is reduced by 1.

This continues for each day until at the start of the 7th day, the character's Max Energy has been reduced by 1 four times, bringing their Max Energy down to 12.

Suppose same character goes without food for 13 days. Because they can go 6 days (their level * 2), without food, it's on the start of the 7th day that character begins to reduce their Max Energy.
