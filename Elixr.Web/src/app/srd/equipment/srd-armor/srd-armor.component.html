Armor

Armor is worn by characters to increase their Defense, making them less likely to be hit. Armor descriptions include: Is Cumbersome and Don Time.

Is Cumbersome

When worn, some armor causes some skills to have Disadvantage due to the armor's weight or awkwardness. These skills include: Athletics, Acrobatics, Climb, Swim, and Stealth

Don Time

Armor takes time to put on. The Don Time is how many rounds it takes to fully put on armor.

The time it takes to remove armor is half of the Don Time.
