Armor

Armor is worn by characters to increase their Defense, making them less likely to be hit. Armor descriptions include: Defense Bonus, Speed Penalty, Agility Penalty.

Defense Bonus

While wearing the given armor, a creature's Defense is increased by an amount equal to the Defense Bonus.

Speed Penalty

A creature's Effective Speed is reduced by the sum of the Speed Penalty for all armor that the creature is wearing.

This penalty is doubled when performing the Swim or Climb skills.

Agility Penalty

A creature's Effective Agility Score is reduced by the sum of the Agility Penalty for all armor that creature is wearing.

Donning Armor

It takes a number of rounds equal to the armor's weight divided by 10, then multiplied by 2. So an armor with a weight of 50 would have a don time of 10 (50 / 2 = 5. 5 * 2 = 10.)

Taking off armor (doffing) takes half as long as donning.
