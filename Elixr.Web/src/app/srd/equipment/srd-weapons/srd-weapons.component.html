Weapons

Weapon's are used to increase a character's effectiveness in combat. Weapon descriptions include: Attack Ability, Damage Ability, Damage, and Range.

Attack Ability

When making an attack roll, a character takes the result of 1d20 + the Bonus of the ability as specified by this property of the weapon. If the result exceeds the target Defense, the character gets to roll Damage against the target. See the combat section of Playing Elixr for more information.

Damage Ability

On a successful attack, damage is rolled according to the damage property of the weapon (see below). Then, the result of the damage roll is increased by the Bonus of the ability as specified by this property of the weapon.

Damage

On a successful a ability as specified by this propety of the weapon. If the result exceeds the target Defense, the character gets to roll Damage against the target.

Range

Indicates how far away from a target a weapon is effective. While the distance to the target is within range, an attacker rolls normally. If the distance is to the target is still within double the range specified, the attacker may still roll, but with Disadvantage.
