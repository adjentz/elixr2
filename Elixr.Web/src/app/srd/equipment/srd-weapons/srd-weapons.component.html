Weapons

Weapon's are used to increase a character's effectiveness in combat. Weapon descriptions include: Attack Ability, Damage Ability, Damage, and Range.

Attack Ability

What physical ability is relevant to the attack roll.

When making an attack roll, a character takes the result of 1d20 + The Modifier of the weapon's attack ability + any misc. modifiers. If the result exceeds the target Defense, the character gets to roll Damage against the target.

Damage Ability

What physical ability is relevant to the damage roll.

Damage

The Damage of a weapon is what is rolled on a successful hit. When a target is hit, they reduce their Energy by the amount of damage rolled + the Modifier of the Damage Ability.

Range

Some weapons have a Range, which is the distance that a weapon can effectively be aimed and fired at a target. There are four range categories: Short, Medium, Long, and Extreme.

Short range is any distance less than or equal to 50ft.

Medium range is any distance less than or equal to 100ft, and greater than 50ft.

Long range is any distance less than or equal to 200ft, and greater than 100ft.

Extreme range is any distance longer than 200ft.

A ranged weapon can be fired one category above its given Range, but attacks are made with Disadvantage.
